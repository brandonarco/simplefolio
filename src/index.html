About me

A highly accomplished MBA scholar-athlete with a strong background in finance, I offer a unique blend of skills and experiences that set me apart. In addition to my MBA, I bring a wealth of expertise from my career as a realtor, where I've honed my abilities in conducting research, analyzing financial data and market trends, and creating intricate financial models for deal evaluation. Beyond my professional accomplishments, I've excelled academically, earning multiple accolades including 2x PAC-12 All-Academic Selection and CoSIDA Academic All-American (Football). Currently, I'm pursuing my passion as a professional football player in the XFL league, where I consistently demonstrate my capacity to remain composed and professional under high-stress situations, exhibit exceptional teamwork, and uphold a strong work ethic. My competitive nature, ability to thrive under pressure, and dedication to teamwork, combined with my strong work ethic, make me an invaluable asset to any organization. I am confident in my ability to contribute to and excel in any industry I choose to pursue. Feel free to "Contact Me" to discuss how I can add value to your team.

My journey began with a curiosity-driven exploration of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the foundational languages that power the web.
